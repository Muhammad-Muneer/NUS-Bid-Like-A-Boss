How to bid like a boss ?

  • Filter Search

    Filters out modules you can take based on timetable, exam, module history (pre-requisites & preclusions) and your module preferences.

    Saves your precious time in choosing modules

  • Bidding Stats

    Graphical representation (instead of the difficult-to-read tables) of bidding statistics across rounds and academic years.

    Helps you to make wise decision during bidding

  • Module Manager

    Overview of your module history

    Helps you keep track of your University requirements such as 1000 level modules, GEM/SS modules and your overall degree requirement
